How to land a job

Network your way into your next position

Use your connections, both past and present to network your into your next position. While speaking with them, whather it be during a Marketing Meeting or a casual conversation, be sure to ask them who else you should speak with.

Design your brand

Ensure your brand matches. When designing your Marketing Sheet, Linked IN profile, resume, etc, ensure that you are sending out the same message. Don't have a Marketing Sheet or and Elevator Pitch that represents you as Customer Service Representive, and a Linked IN Profile designed around Business Administration.
